Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Overview

This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are provided for bookings and payments made at MK HEIGHT hostel.

3. Security Deposit Refund

  • Security deposit is fully refundable upon checkout.
  • Deductions may be made for damages, unpaid dues, or missing items.
  • Refund is processed within 7-10 working days after checkout.
  • Room inspection is mandatory before deposit release.

4. Early Checkout

  • One month's written notice is required for early checkout.
  • If vacating without notice, one month's rent will be forfeited.
  • Pro-rata refund is not available for partial month stays.

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable under any circumstances:

  • One-time admission/registration fees
  • Mess charges for used days
  • Laundry and additional service charges
  • Penalty charges

6. Refund Process

  • Submit refund request in writing to the hostel office.
  • Provide your bank account details for transfer.
  • Refunds are processed via bank transfer only (no cash refunds).
  • Processing time: 7-10 working days after approval.

7. Force Majeure

In case of circumstances beyond our control (natural disasters, government orders, pandemics), a full credit note will be issued for future use, valid for 12 months.
